About Cartigo

We’re on a mission to make style accessible, joyful, and effortless. Meet the people and values behind your favorite destination.

Our story

Cartigo was born in 2020 from a simple idea: shopping should feel like discovering a treasure chest, not a chore. What started as a small pop-up in San Francisco has grown into a curated online destination for fashion-forward individuals who value quality, authenticity, and a little spark of surprise.

We believe that what you wear tells a story — and we’re here to help you write yours. Every product is handpicked by our team of style editors, ensuring that each piece meets our standards for design, durability, and character.

What we stand for

Cartigo is more than a store — it's a community. We partner with independent artists, support local makers, and donate a portion of every sale to organizations advancing education and equity in fashion.

Since 2022, we've planted over 5,000 trees through our partnership with One Tree Planted. Because style shouldn't cost the earth.
